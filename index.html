💌 A Message for You

Words from My Heart

Twenty months of you — and still
I find myself breathless at the gift of it.
Not a choice I'd ever take back,
not a moment I'd trade for silence.

You are the reason I rise with purpose,
the quiet fire behind everything I build —
every dream I chase, I chase toward us,
toward a future I cannot wait to hold.

Yes, we have weathered our storms.
We have bent — but never broken —
because love is not the absence of the fight;
it is choosing each other after.
And every single time, I choose you.

The world is wide, my love,
and I will walk every road of it with you —
from quiet mornings to every city
we haven't yet mapped between us.

My truest words, my deepest vows,
I am saving for the altar on July 10.
But know this, here and now:
You are a miracle I am still learning
how to deserve — and I will never stop trying.

I love you, love.
In every month, in every life.

— Always yours 🤍
